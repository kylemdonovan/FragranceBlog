# Liquid Blossom - Content Ideas & Development Roadmap

## Editorial Content Pipeline

### Beginner's Guide Series
**"I Know Nothing About Fragrance" - A Starter Guide**
- Fragrance families explained (fresh, woody, oriental, floral)
- Understanding notes: top/heart/base structure
- What are gourmands, fougères, chypres?
- Concentration levels: EDT vs EDP vs Parfum
- How to test and choose fragrances
- Building your first collection on a budget
- *Inspired by real conversations with people new to fragrance*

### Deep Dives
- **Provenance matters:** How region, ingredients, and house history shape scent
- **Reformulation reality:** Why your favorite fragrance changed
- **Niche vs. Designer:** What you're actually paying for
- **Seasonal rotation:** Building a year-round wardrobe

## Community Engagement Ideas

### Sample Giveaway Program
- **Format:** Monthly 15ml decant giveaway to random comment winner
- **Timeline:** Winner selected 2 weeks after post publication
- **Logistics needed:**
  - Set up official Liquid Blossom email
  - Create entry/shipping address form
  - Source quality atomizers/decant supplies
  - Draft terms & conditions
- **Legal considerations:** Verify regulations on shipping alcohol-based products
